About the Event

The Climate Action Hackathon 2025 unites students, developers, and community innovators to tackle urban heat and flood challenges. Together, we will co create digital solutions that promote resilience and environmental awareness.

  • Urban heat mitigation using green infrastructure mapping.
  • Flood resilience and early-warning systems.
  • Data driven awareness and visualization tools.

SDG 13: Climate Action

This event supports UN Sustainable Development Goal 13 taking urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. By developing techdriven solutions, participants will contribute to reducing climate risks in urban communities.
